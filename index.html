The Lion And The Wolf



Once Upon A time , there lived a lion named Kesari in a jungle.

He considered himself very Brave.

His friend Feluda jackal was very Clever.



One day a hunter came and thought of hunting the lion a selling his hide and earn Profit.

But jackal was seeing all this.

Next day he said Kesari not to go towards the west of jungle as there is a deer grazing and he is jackal's prey.

Kesari said that the prey would be his and went.

While the jackal turned his way back and put stones in the netand it lifted automatically.
